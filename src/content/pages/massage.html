MASSAGE THERAPY SERVICES

Richard is a certified massage therapist with 26 years of experience, and holds several certifications that are technique specific including: Orthopedic Massage, Sports Massage, Fascial Therapy, Manual Lymph Drainage, and is a Certified Sacroiliac Joint Specialist. Benefit from his expertise in our professional environment, designed to promote relaxation and healing.

  • Orthopedic massage is a therapeutic technique that focuses on alleviating musculoskeletal pain and improving function through targeted manipulation of muscles, tendons, and joints.

  • Sports massage is a specialized form of massage designed to enhance athletic performance, prevent injuries, and aid in recovery by addressing the specific needs of active individuals and athletes.

  • Fascial therapy is a treatment approach that targets the fascia, the connective tissue surrounding muscles and organs, to improve mobility, reduce pain, and enhance overall function by releasing tension and restoring balance.

  • Manual lymph drainage is a gentle massage technique designed to stimulate the lymphatic system, reduce swelling, and promote the removal of toxins and waste from the body.
