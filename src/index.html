Richard's Story

Richard is a Competitive Natural Bodybuilder. However, the road to get there was not easy. On February 28, 2020, he sustained a traumatic brain injury. A couple months later, he succumbed to toxic mold sickness. He lost 50 Ibs. and had itchy, bloody eczema over his entire body. He was later diagnosed with a life threatening Auto Immune Disease.

In a 3 1/2 year period, he survived several hundred life threatening allergic and auto immune reactions. It took him over a year to overcome paralysis and learn how to walk properly again. Yet he soldiered on to become a four time national champion. He is an inspiration to all!
